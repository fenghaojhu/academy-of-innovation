Design + Modeling Instructor

Skyler Zeng

Skyler Zeng is a rising junior attending Dulaney High School, frequently participating in and interested in engineering programs such as the VEX Robotics Competition that Dulaney offers.

Skyler serves as the lead designer, modeler, and constructor of his team's robot throughout 2024-2026, consistently placing top 10 throughout competitions of the 2026 competition.

Just like his role in VEX, Skyler also served as a designer for the NASA DWU 2025-2026 High School Challenge for Team Galaxy. Together, the team designed a cost-efficient drone that offers pest control utilizing AI imagery, allowing them to place 3rd nationwide as the first Maryland team to land top three in the competition's history.

Using this design, the team also participated in the AASF AIX Summit convention, presenting their design on a poster the team created.
