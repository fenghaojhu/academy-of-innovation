FAQ

Questions parents and students may have.

Quick answers about eligibility, format, cost, outcomes, and what happens after the Academy.

Who should apply?

Motivated rising 9th-12th grade students who are curious about engineering, AI, research, entrepreneurship, or STEM competitions.

Do students need prior competition experience?

No. The Academy is built for students who are ready to learn, collaborate, and turn early ideas into stronger project directions.

What does the $100 program cost support?

The fee helps cover meals for the first and last in-person days, room rental, workshop materials, and other program logistics.

What will students leave with?

Students leave with innovation skill sets, presentation practice, mentor feedback, and next-step competition plans they can continue developing after the program.

What is the format?

Students complete asynchronous Innovation 101 work, begin with an in-person launch, meet virtually during the workshop week, and return for final presentations.

How do students get help after the Academy?

The program is designed to point students toward competitions, research directions, and project next steps they can pursue throughout the year.
