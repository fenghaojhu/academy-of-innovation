Design + Modeling Instructor

Skyler Zeng

Skyler Zeng is an engineering-focused student and a rising junior attending Dulaney High School. To take a step into the world of engineering, he participates in programs such as VEX Robotics, serving as the lead designer for his school team 1727V. Modeling the robot using the Autodesk Inventor CAD software, he designs and constructs the robot adapting to each year's challenge, troubleshooting and refining the physical model. This helps further the team's progression towards being competition ready, allowing 1727V to consistently place top 10 at local competitions within Maryland as well as the State competition.

Another program he has participated in is the NASA DWU 2025-2026 High School challenge, serving as a mechanical designer for Team Galaxy. He researched, brainstormed and sketched different mechanisms of the team's drone, with this year's challenge aiding the real-world issue pest management. After the project was finalized, the team achieved a nationwide 3rd place spot on the podium, becoming the first Maryland team in competition history to reach the podium. Using this innovative model, the team also presented their aircraft at the AASF AIX Summit convention, providing a potential solution to a real world issue.
