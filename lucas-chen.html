Lead Instructor

Lucas Chen

Lucas Chen is a 10th-grade student at Eastern Technical High School, Maryland's top-ranked high school. He brings over seven years of programming expertise to the classroom, validated by a perfect score of 5 on the AP Computer Science Principles Exam.

Having initiated his own coding education in Scratch at age nine, Lucas has developed and published 30 independent software projects. He has actively shared this expertise as a summer school Scratch instructor since 2022.

His technical specialty lies in translating software logic into functional engineering solutions; he previously secured 2nd place in the JHU/APL MESA finals by programming an autonomous aquatic trash collection system.

As Team Captain for Team Galaxy, Lucas recently directed the engineering of an autonomous aerial system for precision agriculture, leading his team to a Third Place finish in the 2025-2026 NASA Dream With Us Design Challenge. This historic achievement represents the first national podium placement for a Maryland team in the competition's history.
